Instructions

  • This is the Lagos-version of Tic-Tac-Toe.
  • The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares. It's a two-player game.
  • First player is the Passenger, second player is the Conductor. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
  • The first player to get 3 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
